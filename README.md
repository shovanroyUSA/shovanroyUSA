# 🚀 Hi, I'm Shovan Roy | PhD Scholar @ TTU

I am a PhD Researcher at **Tennessee Tech University (CEROC)** specializing in the intersection of **Agentic AI** and **Zero Trust Architecture (ZTA)**. My work focuses on building secure, autonomous, and privacy-preserving systems for the **Internet of Battlefield Things (IoBT)**.

---

## 🔬 Current Research & Focus
- 🤖 **Agentic Zero Trust (AZTA)**: Architecting dynamic security frameworks where AI agents autonomously orchestrate trust and verification.
- 🧠 **Local & Quantized LLMs**: Implementing privacy-first, offline AI using **Llama-cpp** and quantized models (Mistral-7B, Llama 3) to minimize cloud dependency and cost.
- 📂 **RAG Pipelines**: Developing Retrieval-Augmented Generation workflows to give autonomous agents secure, context-aware knowledge bases.
- 🛡️ **ExplainGuard**: Researching frameworks to secure the explainability supply chain in AI-driven cybersecurity.
